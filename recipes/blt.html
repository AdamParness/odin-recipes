Description

This BLT is classic: bacon, lettuce, and tomato â€” nothing fancy, just delicious.

Ingredients

  • Four Slices of Bacon
  • Two Leaves of Lettuce
  • Two Slices of Tomato
  • Two slices of toasted bread
  • Tablespoon of Mayo

Steps

  1. Cook bacon in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain bacon on a paper towel-lined plate.
  2. Arrange cooked bacon, lettuce, and tomato slices on one slice of bread. Spread mayonnaise on the other slice of bread.
  3. Close to make a sandwich.
