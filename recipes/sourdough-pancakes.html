Sourdough Pancakes

Description

This is a delicious and cakey sourdough pancake that will certainly get you excited for breakfast! Feel free to add any fresh fruit or chocolate chips to the batter.

Ingredients and Recommended Cookware

  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Sourdough Discard (unfed starter)
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Olive Oil
  • Mixing Bowl
  • Whisk
  • Spatula
  • Extra Oil or Butter for cooking
  • Baking Sheet

Steps

  1. Begin by putting two cups of all purpose flour into a mixing bowl
  2. Add two teaspoons of baking powder, one teaspoon of baking soda and one teaspoon of salt and stir
  3. Optional: Two tablespoons of sugar
  4. Put one cup of the sourdough discard, a cup and a half of milk, two tablespoons of olive oil, and a whisked egg into the mixing bowl
  5. Whisk together till smooth, the batter should be thick
  6. Optional:Two teaspoons of vanilla extract or cinnamon
  7. Heat up your griddle or pan and lubricate with butter or oil
  8. Scoop a quarter of a cup of the batter, the pancakes will come out to be five to six inches
  9. Let the pancake for sit for one to two minutes until the pancake is bubble on the top
  10. Flip them over and cook for another minute
  11. Serve your pancakes with fresh fruit and maple syrup
