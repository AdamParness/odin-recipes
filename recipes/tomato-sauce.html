Tomato Sauce

Description

This is a simple but tasty tomato sauce with minimal ingredients. Pictured above is the tomato sauce with gnocchi and chicken.

Ingredients

  • Four to Five Roma Tomatoes
  • One Yellow Onion
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Your Pasta Noodle of Choice

Steps

  1. Blanch the tomatoes in hot water for 5 minutes
  2. In the meantime saute a diced onion with enough olive oil to coat a pan, preferable stainless steel
  3. Strain the tomatoes from the hot water and peel the skin then dice
  4. Put the diced tomatoes in the pan with the sauted onions and add a tablespoon of salt then let the tomatoes simmer till reduced
  5. Blend your sauce with either an immersion blender or a regular blender
  6. Boil your pasta till its ready then strain your pasta and put the pasta in the tomato sauce
  7. Optional: Save some of the pasta water after your pasta is done and put a two to four tablespoons of the starchy water into the sauce
